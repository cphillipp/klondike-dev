Klondike Gourmet™

Red-Skinned, Golden-Fleshed Potatoes

Delicious, small, Klondike potatoes with red skin and yellow flesh. Easy preparation with the small potato that doesn’t have to be peeled and has a creamy, buttery texture that is perfect for any recipe! These potatoes come in a 1.5 lb package makes a perfect side dish for any meal.

  • Unique Characteristics

    Klondike Gourmet™ Red-Skinned Golden-Fleshed potatoes are baby Klondike Rose® potatoes. Their red skin and buttery yellow flesh combine the best of Red and Gold potatoes to give you a richly-flavored, buttery potato at a small, convenient size.

Preparation

Basic Cooking Instructions

Roasting

Cut cleaned potatoes in wedges. Toss them with a mixture of 1 tablespoon (15mL) of melted butter, fresh chopped herbs, salt and pepper. Place in single layer in shallow pan and roast at 425° F (220° C) for 20 to30 minutes or until desired tenderness.

Boiling

In a saucepan, fill with cold water to just over the top of the potatoes. Over medium heat, bring to a boil. Boil for 15 – 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.

Microwaving

Microwave on high for 4 – 5 minutes or until the potatoes are easily pierced with a fork.

Preparation Secrets

Easily Add a Ton of Flavor

If you’re mashing your potatoes, try boiling them in salty water or in chicken broth. This will add flavor to your potatoes as they cook instead of trying to add it after they’ve already been cooked.

How to Get Crispy Baked French Fries

To avoid the high fat of deep-fried french fries, you can bake them instead. To help your fries crisp up, drizzle them in olive oil and toss them in corn starch.
