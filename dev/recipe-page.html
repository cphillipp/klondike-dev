    Yellow-Yellow Fleshed

Roasted Klondike Gourmet™ Potatoes with Rosemary

  • 4 Ratings
Rate & Review
  • Difficulty:
    Easy
  • Prep Time:
    60 Mins
  • Servings:
    6
Ingredients
  • 1 bag Klondike Gourmet™ Yellow-Yellow Fleshed
  • 2-3 sprigs of fresh rosemary coarsely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • This is a fake ingredient name that is really really long to show how ingredients look when they wrap to a second line.
Preparation
  1. 1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C).
  2. 2. In a large bowl, combine oil, garlic, basil, marjoram, dill weed, thyme, oregano, parsley, red pepper flakes, and salt. Stir in potatoes until evenly coated. Place potatoes in a single layer on a roasting pan or baking sheet.
  3. 3. Roast for 20 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, turning occasionally to brown on all sides.
